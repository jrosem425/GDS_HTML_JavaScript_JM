Fun Facts About Wolves

  1. Wolves have powerful jaws capable of exerting a crushing pressure of about 1,500 pounds
  2. Wolves live in packs that typically consist of family units, including parents and their offspring. The pack structure is hierarchical, with an alpha pair usually leading the group
  3. Once wolves mate, they often stay together for life, demonstrating strong bonds and loyalty
